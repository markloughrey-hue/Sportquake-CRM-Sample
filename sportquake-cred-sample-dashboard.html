Context

The Salesforce and Sales Loft totals describe the size of the full clean-up opportunity discussed with Sportquake. They are context only — nothing on this dashboard is extrapolated to them. Every metric is measured on the 1,139-record sample.

Methodology & sources

Source contacts: "SQ – Fintech Spenders Contacts" sample (1,139 rows, 214 companies). Composition, concentration and duplicate figures are computed directly from that file.

Match outcomes: from the CRED matching output — 963 with a senior recommendation (38 already best / 369 upgrade / 525 leaver / 31 company-only), 498 new-org leads, 176 manual review (112 no-roster + 64 no-company). Recommendations are filtered to Director-level and above, with commercial relevance (partnerships / sponsorship / commercial / marketing / sales) as the tiebreaker. Match confidence across the matched set: 359 very-high, 478 high, 202 medium, 63 low. Percentages are rounded.
